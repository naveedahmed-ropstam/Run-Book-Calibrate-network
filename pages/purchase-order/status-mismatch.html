Logiwa Completed but TechOMS still open?

Search in TechOMS and Logiwa · compare lines · pick a fix · re-sync.

Logiwa

Completed — received in warehouse

TechOMS

Sent to Warehouse — not Received

Where to search

TechOMS Logs & PO data
  • Purchasing → Purchase Orders — copy receipt ID · view PO lines
  • Settings → Fulfillment Channels — search receipt ID in channel logs
Logiwa Receipt lines
  • Inbound → Expected Receipts — open PO · compare line items
  • Check item count, SKUs, and quantities vs TechOMS
  • Flag extra lines not exported from TechOMS
🖼️ TechOMS — Fulfillment Channel logs: search by receipt ID → open message for error detail
🖼️ Logiwa — Expected Receipts: line-by-line SKU & qty vs TechOMS PO

Investigation checklist

  • Check Fulfillment Channel logs for error details
  • Verify receipt ID in logs matches PO
  • Compare PO lines in TechOMS vs Logiwa (item count, SKUs, quantities)
  • Identify extra items in Logiwa that don't exist in TechOMS
  • Decide on fix — see internal handling policy (client/retailer: usually keep extra line)
  • Apply fix and retry sync
  • Verify status changed to Received
ℹ️
Client/retailer policy (confirmed)

Qty mismatch → receive actual in Logiwa; client updates Shopify manually (TechOMS PO cannot be edited). Extra line from manufacturer cross-ship → keep on PO #1, do not remove. Why it happens · Full policy.

Sync blocked?

Manual Logiwa lines are not supported by TechOMS — status may not move to Received until reconciled or manually received. See checklist above.
