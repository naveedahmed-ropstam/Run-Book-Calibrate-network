Why this happens — example walkthrough

Example PO-1001 · ABC Manufacturer · Mar 5, 2026

Step 1 ABC Manufacturer · combined shipment
PO #1: 100 Red Shirts ✅ expected PO #2: 10 Blue Pants ❌ early / not on PO #1

Why manufacturers do this: extra stock ready · saves shipping cost · ships PO #2 partially early on PO #1 carton

↓ truck arrives at warehouse
Step 2 Logiwa · WMS → Receive → PO-1001
Red Shirts: 100 ✅ Blue Pants: 10 ❌ extra line

Logiwa records actual received qty · inventory +100 shirts +10 pants · PO marked Received in Logiwa (with extra line)

↓ sync to TechOMS
Result TechOMS · out of sync
Logiwa Completed ≠ TechOMS Sent to Warehouse

Ambiguity #1 — what to do with the extra line?

The problem
  • PO #2 not in TechOMS yet (not sent from Shopify)
  • Cannot create PO #2 manually — missing details
  • 10 Blue Pants are physically here — must receive them
Client/retailer decision
  • Add 10 Blue Pants to PO #1 manually in Logiwa
  • Keep the extra line — do not remove
  • TechOMS PO #1 is locked — cannot add line there

Ambiguity #2 — PO quantity mismatch

Ordered (TechOMS / PO #1)

100 Red Shirts only

Received (Logiwa actual)

100 Red Shirts + 10 Blue Pants

❌ Cannot do
  • Edit TechOMS PO #1 (locked)
  • Remove extra line from Logiwa (client/retailer wants it kept)
  • Auto-sync mismatch back to Shopify
✅ Must do
  • Receive actual qty in Logiwa (100 shirts + 10 pants)
  • Keep extra line on PO #1 in Logiwa
  • Document discrepancy · client updates Shopify manually
ℹ️
Why TechOMS can't fix it

PO is locked after processing · TechOMS does not support manually added Logiwa lines · no automatic PO sync to Shopify. This is a business/process outcome, not a broken integration — follow the policy sections below.

1 — Quantity mismatch

Logiwa receive actual qty → TechOMS PO unchanged 🔒 → Shopify client fixes manually
ℹ️
Can we sync the mismatch to the client?

No. TechOMS POs cannot be edited after lock — there is no automatic PO qty sync back to the client. Receive the physical quantity in Logiwa; client/retailer must update their PO manually in Shopify on their side.

  • Receive actual quantity in Logiwa (short/over as physically counted)
  • Document discrepancy in ticket — ordered vs received
  • Notify client that TechOMS PO qty will not auto-update
  • Client updates PO in Shopify manually
  • Qty mismatch alone may not block receipt sync — still note it

2 — Extra line (manufacturer cross-ship)

PO #1 ships early + item from PO #2 on same carton → Logiwa manual line on PO #1
✅
Client/retailer policy — keep the line

Extra lines only cause sync issues on integration POs (TechOMS-managed). It is common for manufacturers to ship an item from another open PO on the wrong shipment. If PO #2 is not in the system yet, the only way to receive that physical unit is to add it manually to PO #1 in Logiwa. The client/retailer has asked the team to keep it on PO #1 — do not remove it.

⚠️
Why TechOMS stays out of sync

TechOMS does not support manually added Logiwa lines — status may stay Sent to Warehouse until lines are reconciled or manually received. This is expected when cross-ship items are received before PO #2 exists.

  • Confirm extra SKU was physically on the shipment (not a receiving mistake)
  • Check if item belongs to another open PO not yet in system
  • If client/retailer directed add-to-PO-1 — keep line, do not delete
  • Document which PO the item will eventually belong to
  • Follow status mismatch checklist if sync blocked
