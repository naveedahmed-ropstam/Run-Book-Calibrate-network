Critical Screens Reference

Key TechOMS, Logiwa, and Rithum screens every support analyst must know — what each is for and when to use it.

TechOMS Screens

Sales Orders List

Navigation: Sales → Sales Orders

Purpose: Search and view all orders across channels. Primary starting point for order issues.

When to use: Customer inquiry, missing order, status check, hold investigation.

Key fields: Channel order ID, status, hold reason, fulfillment channel, ship-by date.
