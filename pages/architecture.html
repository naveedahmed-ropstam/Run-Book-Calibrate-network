System Architecture

Calibrate Networks is a third-party logistics (3PL) partner. We receive customer inventory into our warehouse, store and manage it, pick and pack orders, ship through carriers, and keep order and inventory data in sync with each customer’s sales channels and marketplaces.

As a 3PL we do not own the products — our customers do. Our job is reliable fulfillment plus accurate inventory, on-time ship confirmations, and compliant messaging for retail and EDI partners. The sections below show how that works across Logiwa (warehouse), TechOMS (orchestration), and Rithum (marketplace & EDI).

System Roles

Logiwa WMS · what is physically in the building
  • Receiving · putaway
  • On-hand inventory truth
  • Pick · pack · ship
  • Returns receiving
  • Cycle counts
TechOMS OMS · traffic controller
  • Receives orders (channel / Rithum)
  • Routing & allocation rules
  • Fulfillment coordination
  • Status & inventory sync
  • Import / export jobs
Rithum Marketplace & EDI hub
  • Retailer / marketplace connectivity
  • EDI translation & compliance
  • Inventory feeds to retail
  • ASN & retailer messaging
Sales Channels Demand sources · may connect direct, via TechOMS, or Rithum
  • Shopify · Amazon · Walmart
  • eBay · WooCommerce · Target
  • Order capture · storefront content
Carriers Transport · invoked from Logiwa at pack/ship
  • Labels · rating
  • Tracking events
  • Proof of delivery
