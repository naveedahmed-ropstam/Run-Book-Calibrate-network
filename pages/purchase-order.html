What fires on each step — outbound (create & send) then inbound (receive & close). See also Fulfillment Jobs guide · Integration sync schedules.

Phase A — Create & send to warehouse
1
Trigger PO status → Ordered Client/retailer marks PO and sends to supplier
Shopify channel notification (PO update webhook) → TechOMS Sales Channel

Fires Inbound export job On PO approval — pushes expected receipt to Logiwa via Fulfillment Channel
TechOMS → Logiwa API (on PO approval · ~seconds)

Result Sent to Warehouse External ID stored · awaiting receipt confirmation

Phase B — Receive & close the loop
5
Trigger Receiving completed Warehouse finishes put-away · receipt marked complete
Logiwa receipt complete → TechOMS inbound confirmation (near real-time + 15 min retry)
6
Fires Receipt match & status update Match by receipt ID & SKU lines · PO → Received if lines align
Result Completed On-hand inventory updated in Logiwa
7
Fires Inventory / ATS sync TechOMS → Shopify every 15–30 min (sellable qty update)
Result PO Received · ATS refreshed Client/retailer may still update Shopify PO status manually

Debugging tip

For receipt issues, search the receipt ID in TechOMS Fulfillment Channel logs — not general job logs. See status mismatch walkthrough.

Three Steps

1 🛍️

Shopify Create PO

Ordered → notify TechOMS

User creates PO · supplier & SKUs set · triggers inbound workflow.

2 ⚙️

TechOMS Lock & send

Store → Lock → → Logiwa

PO frozen (qty · SKUs · supplier) · forwarded to warehouse.

3 📥

Logiwa Prepare receipt

Incoming Shipment → Pending Receipt

Warehouse sees expected qty · receiving can begin at dock.

⚠️
PO locked in TechOMS

No edits to qty, SKUs, or supplier after processing. Coordinate changes with client before ordering.
