Warehouse Systems Glossary

Plain-language definitions of WMS, OMS, EDI, and CN terms used across TechOMS, Logiwa, Rithum, and Shopify operations.

3

CN
Third-Party Logistics provider. A company that handles warehousing, fulfillment, and shipping on behalf of brands/clients.

8

810
EDI Invoice. Bills the retailer for shipped merchandise.
846
EDI Inventory Advice. Periodic inventory availability feed to retailers/marketplaces.
850
EDI Purchase Order. Retailer order sent to supplier/CN. Creates a sales order in TechOMS.
855
EDI PO Acknowledgment. Response accepting, rejecting, or changing PO lines.
856
EDI Advance Ship Notice (ASN). Notifies retailer of shipment with tracking and carton details.
870
EDI Order Status. Status updates and cancellation requests/responses.

9

940
EDI Warehouse Shipping Order. Optional document instructing WMS to fulfill (often replaced by API).
945
EDI Warehouse Shipping Advice. Optional WMS ship confirmation back to OMS (often replaced by API).

A

Allocation
Reserving specific inventory (location + qty) against a shipment order for picking.
ASN
Advance Ship Notice. Same as EDI 856. Critical for retailer compliance.
ATS
Available-to-Sell. Quantity that can be sold on channels. Typically on-hand minus reservations, safety stock, and holds.

B

Backorder
Order line accepted but cannot ship until inventory is available.
Blind Ship
Shipment where retailer branding is used but CN fulfills. Common in dropship.
BOL
Bill of Lading. Shipping document for freight/LTL inbound shipments.

C

Cancel-By
Retailer deadline to accept or reject a PO. Missing this may auto-cancel the order.
Chargeback
Financial penalty from retailer for compliance failure (late ship, missing ASN, etc.).
Cycle Count
Periodic physical count of inventory in specific locations to verify system accuracy.

D

Dropship
Fulfillment model where the CN ships directly to the end consumer on behalf of a retailer.
DTC
Direct-to-Consumer. Sales through brand-owned channels like Shopify, not through retailers.

E

EDI
Electronic Data Interchange. Standardized business documents (850, 856, etc.) exchanged between trading partners.

F

FEFO
First Expired, First Out. Allocation strategy for products with expiry dates.
FIFO
First In, First Out. Allocation strategy shipping oldest inventory first.
Fulfillment Channel
TechOMS configuration linking to a WMS (Logiwa) for order export and ship confirm.

I

IntegratedFlow
TechOMS connector framework for API integrations between systems.

L

LTL
Less Than Truckload. Freight shipping for palletized inbound shipments.

M

Multi-Client
Logiwa capability to segregate inventory and operations by client/brand in one warehouse.

O

OMS
Order Management System. Orchestrates orders across sales channels and fulfillment locations. TechOMS is our OMS.
On-Hand
Physical inventory quantity in the warehouse. Logiwa is the system of record for on-hand.
Oversell
Selling more units than physically available. Usually caused by inventory sync lag.

P

Pick Task
Directed instruction to retrieve specific SKU/qty from a specific location.
PO
Purchase Order. Inbound order to vendor for replenishment stock.
Putaway
Moving received inventory from staging to storage/pick locations.

Q

Quarantine
Non-pickable location for returns, damage, or holds pending inspection.

R

Reservation
Soft hold on inventory at OMS level to prevent overselling before WMS hard allocation.
RMA
Return Merchandise Authorization. Approved return record linking to original order.
Routing Guide
Retailer-specific rules for carriers, labels, packaging, and ASN format.

S

Safety Stock
Buffer quantity withheld from channel ATS to prevent overselling during sync lag.
Sales Channel
TechOMS configuration linking to a demand source (Shopify, Rithum).
SCAC
Standard Carrier Alpha Code. 2-4 letter carrier identifier used in EDI (e.g. UPS, FDXE).
Ship-By
Date by which order must ship to meet retailer SLA.
Short Ship
Shipping fewer units than ordered. Requires approval and status update on remaining qty.
SKU
Stock Keeping Unit. Unique identifier for a product variant used across all systems.
SLA
Service Level Agreement. Contracted timeframes for ship, ack, ASN, etc.
SSCC
Serial Shipping Container Code. 18-digit barcode on cartons (UCC-128) required by many retailers.

T

Trading Partner
A retailer or marketplace connected via EDI/Rithum with unique ID and requirements.

U

UCC-128
Barcode label standard for retail cartons and pallets. Required on ASN.
UPC
Universal Product Code. Barcode on product packaging; may differ from internal SKU.

W

Wave
Group of shipment orders released together for picking in the warehouse.
WISMO
Where Is My Order. Customer inquiry about order status — usually needs tracking lookup.
WMS
Warehouse Management System. Manages physical inventory and warehouse operations. Logiwa is our WMS.
